5-Day San Diego Theme Adventure

LEGOLAND, SeaWorld, and California Coast Highlights

Day 1: Arrival & Coastal Setup

Morning/Afternoon: Travel & Check-in

  • **Activity:** Arrive at San Diego International Airport (SAN).
  • **Activity:** Drive North (approx. 45 mins) to your hotel, preferably in **Carlsbad**.
Find Carlsbad, CA (Recommended Base)

Evening: Dinner & Early Rest

  • **Activity:** Head to **Carlsbad Village** for a relaxed, casual dinner.
  • **Activity:** Prep park bags and get plenty of sleep for the two big LEGOLAND days ahead!

Day 2: LEGOLAND - Main Rides & Ninjago

Navigate to LEGOLAND California

Priority Activities (Morning)

  • **Rides:** Immediately hit the popular coasters: **The Dragon Coaster** (Castle Hill) and **Coastersaurus** (Land of Adventure).
  • **Interactive:** Experience **LEGO Ninjago The Ride**, an immersive 4D adventure where you control the action.

Afternoon Focus

  • **Fun Town:** Let the kids get their "license" at the **Driving School** (ages 6-13) or Junior Driving School (ages 3-5).
  • **Build:** Spend time at the **Build-N-Test** area to design and race LEGO cars.
  • **Show:** Check the schedule for a fun, energetic live show in the park.

Day 3: LEGOLAND - Water, Sea Life & Miniland

Priority Activities (Morning)

  • **Aquarium:** Visit the **SEA LIFE Aquarium** for up-close encounters with ocean creatures and educational talks.
  • **Seasonal Fun:** If open, cool off and play at the **LEGOLAND Water Park** (a separate ticket/combo is usually required).

Afternoon Focus

  • **Miniland USA:** Take a slow walk through the incredible **Miniland USA** displays featuring cities like New York, San Francisco, and Las Vegas, all made from millions of LEGO bricks.
  • **Shopping:** Browse the massive selection at **The BIG Shop** at the park entrance for souvenirs and take-home LEGO sets.

Day 4: SeaWorld San Diego

Navigate to SeaWorld San Diego

Morning: Shows & Encounters

  • **Shows:** Head straight to the major animal presentations, like the **Dolphin Adventures** show. Check the daily schedule immediately upon entering.
  • **Exhibits:** Visit the **Turtle Reef** and the **Aquaria: World of Fishes** areas.

Afternoon: Thrills & Exploration

  • **Rides:** Get your adrenaline fix on the park's coasters: **Manta**, **Electric Eel**, or the hyper-coaster **Emperor**.
  • **Walk-throughs:** Take a chilling walk through the **Shark Encounter** underwater tunnel and watch the hilarious penguins at the **Penguin Encounter**.

Evening: Coastal Fare

  • **Dinner:** Enjoy a final family dinner in the lively **Mission Beach** area before preparing for departure.

Day 5: Departure

Morning: Farewell San Diego

  • **Activity:** Enjoy a final, relaxed breakfast at your hotel.
  • **Activity:** Check-out and head south to the airport.
Navigate to San Diego International Airport (SAN)
Tip: Be sure to check the operating hours and show schedules for both parks the night before your visit.
